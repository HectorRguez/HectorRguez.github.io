📋 Annotation Guidelines

When comparing responses, consider the following criteria:

  • The most important goal of manually labeling is to generate meaningful comparisons. In case of doubt, always discard the pair.
  • Natural reading flow. The advertisement should feel like a seamless part of the response, not an interruption. When responses are very similar in quality, focus on which maintains better contextual flow.
  • Preserve completeness. The original response's helpfulness and information must remain fully intact. If the advertisement changes the response's meaning or helpfulness, reject the pair.
  • Smooth transitions. The product mention should integrate naturally within existing sentence structure. Avoid responses with abrupt topic changes or obvious commercial insertions.
  • Contextual relevance. The product mention should relate meaningfully to the user's question and add apparent value, not feel forced or unrelated.
